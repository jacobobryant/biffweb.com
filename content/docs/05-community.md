This is a list of public apps and libraries that have been built by the Biff community. If you've made something with/for Biff, send me a link.

*   [Schroedingberg/romance-progression](https://github.com/Schroedingberg/romance-progression): "A workout tracker for hypertrophy training."
*   [com.ractiveware/biff-plugins-auth](https://github.com/momerath42/biff-plugins-auth): "third-party auth plugins for biff; currently one monolith with optional password and invite-code support"
*   [Terms Overload](https://termsoverload.com/): "Ask questions about your favorite Terms and Conditions of online webpages!"
*   [SpicyWOD](https://spicywod.com/): "Track and log workouts, PRs, cycles, and follow progressions all in the same place." [Source code](https://github.com/theianjones/spicy-wod).
*   [beij.ing](https://beij.ing): "Think of it as GitHub contributions for tracking improvements in arbitrary skills."
